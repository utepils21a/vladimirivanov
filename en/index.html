Why do we binge at night and “fail” diets?
No, you’re not weak-willed. That’s just how our brain works. When you torture yourself with workouts or starvation, your psyche goes into defense mode — and you end up eating, smoking, and scrolling reels all at once because your brain needs compensation.

This is called the REWARD SYSTEM — and it’s stronger than your goal list for the year.

Don’t fight yourself. Build your system so you enjoy your new habits: strength training, tastier but lighter meals, the feeling of control — this works.
